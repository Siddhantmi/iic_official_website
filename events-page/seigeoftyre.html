Introduction

Siege Of Tyre is a fun and exciting competitive event organized by Innovation and Incubation cell under INNOVANZA 3.0 for the young innovators. It’ll provide them with a platform to transform their ideas into a working model of a “TREBUCHET”.


Flow of Event


Team building:
The participants will be allowed to interact and make teams. Each team can have 4-5 members.

Design submission:
All the teams have to prepare a proper design of their models according to dimensional specifications given. Design can be on any CAD software or on sheet(Scanned copy of Handmade design). Submission of designs will close by 6:00 PM, 3/04/2022(Sunday).

Shortlisting:
On the basis of designs submitted, teams will be shortlisted for the final event.

   Note: The dimensions must be within the provided specifications. The uploaded document should be proper (Neat, Clean and Visible).

Doubt Session:
Each team will be allowed to ask their questions from the event coordinators (say about the event and making of their model). The session will be in online mode, from 2:00 to 4:00 PM, 04/04/2022(Monday).

Working Time:
The teams are supposed to work on their model after getting shortlisted. In this time the participants are supposed work and complete their model and send a video of the working model of the trebuchet by 6:00 PM, 09/04/2022(Saturday).

Reporting:
Teams have to report to the Open-Air Theater (OAT) by 2:45 PM,10/04/2022 with their model on the day of event on time. The team will be provided the time slots for their physical demonstration.

Demonstration:
The teams within their slots will load their trebuchet and launch the test weights while standing on the launch pad. The counter weights and tests weights will be provided by us.

Judging:
The models made by each team will be judged on the basis of different criterions

Prize distribution:
The jury will decide the winner teams in accordance to set parameters and they will be awarded.

Who can Participate?


Higher Secondary school and Undergraduate college students from any Indian college, with basic knowledge of mechanics and usage of Cutting & drilling equipment.

Materials Required


Wooden pieces, screws and nut bolts, rubber band, ropes and cloth piece, Wood cutters and drill machines.
(ALL THE MATERIALISTIC REQUIRMENTS WILL BE FROM TEAMSIDE)

Dimentional Specifications


Height of the trebuchet: 30 cm (max)
Width of base: 30 cm (max)
Length of firearm: 40 cm(max)
Length of back end of the firing arm should be 4 - 6 times the length of front end.
    (Front-end length + Back-end length = length of firing arm)

Judging Criteria


The launch pad will be provided on the day of event.
Judging criteria for the TREBUCHET made by the teams will be:
-loading time (20%)
-distance travelled by the balls of different wights (40%)
-Accuracy while aiming at the targets (40%) (2 Chances only).


Rules and Regulations


1. Each team should have minimum 3 and maximum 5 members.
2. Each team must be given a name as chosen by its members. Team names must not be connected in any way with the names of companies or registered trademarks.
3. If information provided during online registration is found to be incomplete and/or inaccurate, even if related to just one team member, the entire team concerned will be excluded from the competition.
4. Teams will have to keep the deadlines in mind. Extension beyond deadline will lead to disqualification.
5. All participants should maintain discipline and follow the instructions by the organizing team.
6. Final decision for results will be with the jury. No objection regarding results will be entertained.
7. Participants must be studying in class 9th or higher.
8. All participants must carry their institute identity cards at all time during event.
9. All teams from school should be accompanied by teacher assigned by the school.
