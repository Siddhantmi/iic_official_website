Introduction

“There is one thing the photograph must contain, the humanity of the moment.”


PHOTOGRAPHY (PICXELLENCE): Photography is the art or process of clicking pictures or freezing the moment.
VIDEOGRAPHY(VIDMORA): Videography is an art and like any art, it's a way of expressing your ideas, your style, and your way of seeing the world. Also, like any art, it's judged differently by each person who watches your videos.

Theme

“Photography is a way of feeling, of touching, of loving. What you have caught on film is captured forever... It remembers little things, long after you have forgotten everything.”


THEME FOR PHOGRAPHY EVENT:
1. Related to Innovation and Creativity.
2. INNOVANZA 3.0 (Photo can be clicked during the events.)
3. Open.
THEME FOR VIDEOGRAPHY EVENT:
1. Related to Innovation and Creativity.
2. Funny or Motivational.
3. INNOVANZA 3.0 (Video can be shot during the events.)
4. Open.

Event Structure


Registration:
Participants need to register through the registration form which will be released. The individual/group will need to fill the form and required details.

Submission:
Submission of photo and video will be done online through Submission form which will be released after registration of event closes.

Evaluation:
Entries will be judged by the experts in the field of photography and cinematography.

Result Declaration/Award Distribution
After the completion of working time, the teams will be called for the final presentation of their stable structure.

Rules and Regulations


PHOTOGRAPHY (PICXELLENCE):
- This is an individual fun event for the enthusiasts in the field.
- Here the participant has to click a picture either by phone or DSLR.
- The candidate has to write a quote depicting the above clicked picture.
- Quote may be in Hindi or English.
- Clicked picture must be original and clicked after 5th April, 2022 (EXIF file may also be asked during judgement). and clicked after 5th April, 2022.
- The word limit for the quote is 25-30 words.
- The picture must necessarily have the time-stamp on it.
- The picture should be clicked inside the college campus.
- The deadline for submission is 10 April, 5 pm.
VIDEOGRAPHY(VIDMORA):
- This is an individual/group event.
- The participant has to record a short video/reel.
- The video/reel must be 30s – 60 seconds.
- The content in the video should not be casteist, disrespectful, abusive, racist, sexiest, vulgar in any sense.
- Shot Video should be original and shot after 5th April, 2022.
- In this event video editing skill will also be judged (if you are doing any type of editing).
- Video/reels should be recorded inside the college premises only.
- The deadline for the event is 10 April, 7 pm.
