OUR VISION

1. To conduct various innovation and entrepreneurship-related activities in time bound fashion.
2. Identify and reward innovations and share success stories.
3. Organize periodic workshops/ seminars/ interactions with entrepreneurs, investors, professionals and create a mentor pool for student innovators.
4. Network with peers and national entrepreneurship development organizations.
5. Create an Institution’s Innovation portal to highlight innovative projects carried out by institution’s faculty and students.
6. Organize Hackathons, idea competition, mini challenges etc. with the involvement of industries.

OUR MISSION

IIC will focus on creating complete ecosystem which will foster the culture of Innovation across all educational institutions from ideas generation to pre-incubation, incubation and graduating from the incubator as successful start-ups. IIC will actively engage various central and state agencies to ensure that dedicated budget is allocated for innovation related activities. Ministry of HRD has established ‘MRHD’s Innovation Cell’ with the mandate to work closely with our Incubation Center to encourage the creative energy of our student population to work on new ideas and innovation and promote them to create start-ups and entrepreneurial ventures.

What is INNOVANZA 3.0 ?

Innovation and Incubation Cell, BIET Jhansi has been conducting INNOVANZA as an institute-wide annual technical fest for the last two years. Technical fests are the events that are essential to unveil the technical skills of young students. Participating in technical events helps the students to have a test of their competence and these events test the caliber of engineering students as well. Considering the value of these events, IIC, BIET Jhansi proposes to organize INNOVANZA 3.0 in April. Apart from BIET students, participation from other higher education institutes and schools in Jhansi is also expected. We have curated a plethora of events to unleash the technical skills of young minds. These technical events make a core memory in the minds of young students, and we have kept this in mind. From Hackathon to Robo Race, Quiz to Circuits, we have designed a myriad number of events, to inculcate the technical and communication skills in students. These contests will not only be a testament to their technical caliber but also their ability to perform under time and resource constraints.

Who Conducts INNOVANZA 3.O?

INNOVANZA 3.O is conducted by Innovation and Incubation Cell, BIET JHANSI.

Who can Participate in INNOVANZA 3.O

Students from schools and colleges of Jhansi can participate in this event.

What are the prizes of INNOVANZA 3.O?

There are many exciting prizes for winners in INNOVANZA 3.0 like Cash Prizes, Coupons, Goodies, assured certificates and much more other surprizes are waiting for you.

Is there any participation fee in INNOVANZA 3.O?

Participation in INNOVANZA 3.O is absolutely free.
