Introduction

“Launch and learn, everything is progress.”


Inspired from these words Innovation & Incubation Cell presents “Shuttle Gateway”, a fun rocket launching event. In this event, participants have to build and launch their rocket models. All the materials required for this event will be provided to the participants along with the launching pad. There is no need to bring anything except for the plastic bottle.

Theme

“Small beginnings are the launching pad to great victories.”

The teams will undergo three stages as the following:
a) Bidding
b) Testing
c) Aiming

Bidding: In this, each team leader will be given equal money which they will use for bidding. After the bidding the team will be rewarded with points on the basis of money saved by them.

Testing: Here, each team will test their rockets for height and range of the flight in order to understand the pressure and fuel to be used. The more the height, the more points will be rewarded to the team.

Aiming: Each team will have three rounds to hit a target whose distance will increase after each round. The points will be rewarded accordingly.

For reference about the setup and launching ideas, visit: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SME6ztIS3BA

Event Structure


Registration:
Participants need to register through the registration form which will be released on the Innovation & Incubation Cell website. The teams will need to fill their team leader and other members details. As it is a fun event, the registration is completely free of cost and solely for enjoyment and educative purpose.

Short Listing:
There is no shortlisting. Everyone who is interested to participate is welcome to participate in the event.

Evaluation:
Judgement will be made on the following criteria by jury members which will be from academia.
Marking Scheme:
- Bidding-15%
- Testing-25%
- Aiming-30%
- Time of flight-30%

Result Declaration/Award Distribution
Based on the decision of jury, results will be announced and the winner and runner up team will be rewarded with goodies.

Rules and Regulations


1. Each team can have a maximum of 3 members.
2. Participants will not be allowed to use their cellphones during the event.
3. All participants should maintain discipline and they must follow the given instructions.
4. Final decision for results will be with the jury. No objection regarding results will be entertained.
5. Students from any recognized institution or college can participate.
6. All participants must carry their institute identity cards at all time during event.
7. The team will be disqualified if their materials and fuel gets over.
