Introduction

A transformer is a device that transfers electric energy from one alternating- current circuit to one or more other circuits, either increasing (Stepping up) or reducing (stepping down) the voltage. Transformers are widely used in electrical equipments. So as an engineering student, it becomes very important to know the properties and functions of a transformer. After participating in this event participants will have a brief idea on how to increase the efficiency and utilize the transformer to its full potential.


Flow of Event


Registration:
The registration form will be available, wherein the team needs to register for the event.

Demonstration:
A brief description about the event will be given to all the teams for their better understanding of the event, along with clearing their doubts. .

Fabrication:
The teams will be given 24 hours time slot to make the transformer.

Evaluation:
The transformer made by the teams will be evaluated by the organizing team for the output produced. The team with output closest to the proposed output will be declared winner.

Result declaration and prize distribution ceremony:
The team which prepares the best transformer will be announced as the winner and will be awarded.

Who can Participate?


Students from any recognized college or institute can participate.

Rules and Regulations


  1. 1. Each team may have 3-5 members.
  2. 2. Each team will be given 24 hours to make their own transformer.
  3. 3. In the event only the finished model will be presented.
  4. 4. Participants will be judged on the basis of their different performances of the transformers and neatness in their presentation.
  5. 5. During the presentation, questions will be asked from every member of the team.
  6. 6. The transformer must be made by the participant, if it is found that the model has been purchased, it will lead to immediate disqualification.
  7. 7. The decision of the organizing will be final. No participant should argue with the organizing team.
  8. 8. Participants are required to maintain discipline and decorum during the event.
Transformer Specification:

It will be a step-down transformer of rating 220/15 V.

Judging Criteria:

The winner will be decided on the basis of their transformer performance. Your transformer shall be judged according to speed and accuracy held during the spot round.
