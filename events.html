DECODING TECHNOVATORS MIND: Accelerate Your Idea to a Start-up
Every invention starts with a problem. Engineers follow an iterative design process and creative thinking to address the problem by developing a cycle of ideas. In this fast-growing generation of young innovators, this problem-solving mentality is what empowers them to question things, challenge things and then arrive at a solution that is both technically and commercially viable. This panel discussion aims to bring to life inspiration from Sir James Dyson's life and explores the opportunities that the world of Engineering offers to make a difference through designs that employ clever yet simple engineering principles and address clear problems. With this backdrop, Dyson brings to you a very interesting and interactive webinar with different perspectives technology and digital innovations from both academic and industry experts.
